About Me

            My name is Bennett Tan. I am a first year student who is currently based in Coquitlam, Canada and is keen in majoring in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia (UBC). Technology has always fascinated me from a young age, however, I never considered learning about the industry as I always believed that I could never see programming as anything but daunting.

            After taking some time out of school to serve Singapore's mandatory National Service, I came to the realization that I've never given Computer Science a fair chance as a possible field I would like to study. With my friend's encouragement, I took a chance with an introductory ComSci course at UBC and immediately fell in love with the creative, yet logical aspect to programming.
